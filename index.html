The Problem

Millions of tons of food are wasted every year because people forget expiration dates or lose track of groceries.

Our Solution

SmartFridge reminds users before food expires and helps organize groceries in one simple mobile app.

Our Story

From a Simple Problem to a Smarter Solution

SmartFridge was created to make everyday food management easier, more affordable and more sustainable.

01 — The Idea

Food should be enjoyed, not forgotten.

The idea for SmartFridge began with a familiar everyday situation: groceries are purchased with good intentions, but busy routines make it easy to forget what is already stored in the refrigerator.

Food expires unnoticed, money is wasted and perfectly usable products are thrown away. We wanted to create a simple tool that helps people stay organized without adding more stress to their daily lives.

Less Waste Use groceries before they expire.
More Savings Avoid unnecessary repeat purchases.
Smarter Habits Make sustainable choices every day.
